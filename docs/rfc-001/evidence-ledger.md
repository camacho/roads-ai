# RFC 001 Evidence Ledger

What each externally checkable claim in [RFC 001](./README.md) and its companion [RFC 002](../rfc-002-model-selection.md) rests on. Each row: the claim, its primary source with an exact locator, and what that source does and does not support. Stakeholder constraints, design choices, and labeled hypotheses are not evidence claims and are not listed.

_Checked 2026-07-23. E01–E28 received an author pass plus three independent blind reviews; E29 was separately validated from retained local artifacts; E30–E33 were checked against current primary documentation and closure-reviewed by both agents. Verification methodology and per-row recheck procedures: [workspace/evidence-audit.md](./workspace/evidence-audit.md)._
